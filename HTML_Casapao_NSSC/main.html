Educational Philosophy

The NEW SINAI SCHOOL AND COLLEGES STA. ROSA, INC. is committed to create holistic formation of the human person and produce quality graduates who will join the country’s strong professionals to answer the educational goals of the country.

Vision

NEW SINAI SCHOOL AND COLLEGES STA. ROSA, INC., endeavors to become a premiere educational institution in developing holistic formation of its graduates to be globally competitive.

Mission

NEW SINAI SCHOOL AND COLLEGES STA. ROSA, INC., aims to produce competent professionals, promote academic excellence among students and faculty through the provision of quality education programs for personal, social, spiritual, psychological, cultural and physical development regardless of race, age, gender, religion, political affiliation and socio-cultural state.
